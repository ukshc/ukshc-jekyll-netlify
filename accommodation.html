Bristol has a wide range of accommodation, and most city centre hotels are within easy walking distance of the Trinity Centre. The Trinity Centre is also located on several bus routes. We expect you will want to do your own research into where to stay, but here are some suggestions of hotels and hostels close to the venue to get you started! All distances are given as walking distances; driving may be longer.

Hotels

  • Bristol Marriott Hotel City Centre (0.5 miles)
  • Future Inn Bristol (0.5 miles)
  • Hotel Ibis Bristol Temple Meads Quay (0.7 miles)
  • Hilton Garden Inn Bristol City Centre (0.7 miles)
  • Hotel Novotel Bristol City Centre (0.8 miles)
  • Travelodge Bristol Central Mitchell Lane Hotel (0.9 miles)
  • Premier Inn Bristol City Centre King Street (1 mile)
  • Hotel du Vin (1.1 miles)
  • Brooks Guesthouse Bristol (1.2 miles)

Hostels

  • The Full Moon Backpacker Hotel and Attic Bar (0.8 miles)
  • Rock & Bowl Motel (0.9 miles)
  • YHA Bristol (1.3 miles)
